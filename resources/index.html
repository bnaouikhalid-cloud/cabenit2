01Measurement guide

Capture the room before choosing cabinets.

  1. Sketch every wall.Mark doors, windows, openings and ceiling height.
  2. Measure in inches.Record total wall lengths, then verify smaller segments add up.
  3. Locate utilities.Note plumbing, electrical, vents and gas connections.
  4. Record appliances.Include manufacturer, model and exact installation dimensions.
  5. Photograph the space.Wide views and close details help explain obstacles a sketch may miss.
Measure twice.

A design plan does not replace final field measurements. Confirm all order dimensions with your designer before purchase.

02Assembly guide

Match the method to the product specification.

Assembly can vary by cabinet and interior option. Review the exact product specification and supplied instructions before beginning.

  • Confirm the product SKU and all carton labels.
  • Inventory panels, shelves and hardware before assembly.
  • Work on a clean, padded, level surface.
  • Keep doors and drawer fronts protected until installation.
  • Stop if drilled locations or hardware do not match the instructions.

The featured 18″ wall cabinet specification lists cam-lock assembly for the premium white interior and L-brackets for the UV wood interior.

03Shipping

Confirm method, cost and delivery responsibilities.

Shipping options depend on the destination and order. Available methods and costs are confirmed after the delivery address is entered.

  • Review the delivery method and total before payment.
  • Confirm who must be present and what access the carrier requires.
  • Inspect cartons according to the current delivery instructions.
  • Keep order, SKU and shipment references together when requesting support.

Contact Customer Support for current shipping and availability details.

04Returns

Review the current policy before ordering.

Cabinet return eligibility may depend on product condition, timing, order status and policy exclusions.

  • Confirm each size, variation and SKU before checkout.
  • Keep packaging and documentation until the order is fully checked.
  • Contact support before sending any cabinet or component back.

05Warranty

Review coverage and claim requirements.

Warranty details can vary by product, including coverage, exclusions, proof requirements and claim steps.

  • Keep the order number and product SKUs.
  • Document the issue clearly with photographs.
  • Do not alter or dispose of affected parts before receiving instructions.
